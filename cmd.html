Profile of Prof. Yussuf Abdullah D.

Introduction

PROF. YUSSUF, ABDULLAH D. (MB;BS, FMCPsych., Cert. Health Plan. & Mgt., Cert. Health Inform. Mgt., MCH, FAPA, FIIA) CHIEF MEDICAL DIRECTOR (CMD)/CHIEF EXECUTIVE OFFICER (CEO), UNIVERSITY OF ILORIN TEACHING HOSPITAL (UITH), ILORIN

Personal Information

Birth Date: January 3, 1963
Place of Birth: Moro Local Government Area, Kwara State, Nigeria
Primary Education: Army Children School, Ikeja Lagos
Secondary Education: New State High School, Mushin, Lagos
University Education: University of Nigeria, Nsukka (MB; BS Degree)

Educational Qualifications

  • Certificates in Health Planning and Management (University of Ilorin, 2010-2011)
  • Masters of Community Health (MCH) - University of Ilorin, 2015
